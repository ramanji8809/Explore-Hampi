About Hampi Explorer

Welcome to Hampi Explorer, your ultimate guide to discovering the timeless beauty and rich heritage of Hampi, Karnataka. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Hampi is a land of ancient ruins, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant cultural traditions.

Why Visit Hampi?

Hampi, once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, is a treasure trove of history, architecture, and spirituality. The city is filled with majestic temples, intricate carvings, stone chariots, and grand palaces that tell the story of a glorious past.
